<Section id="1.0.00" title='Problem'>
  <Fragment slot="context">
    Wavy’s homepage featured a single calendar view showing *events*, *notable days* and *suggestions*. The issue was that it was **hard to find events you cared about**.

    - Items were undiffereciated, making hard to discern events from suggestions or find invites & RSVPs. 
    - Calendar only showed one month at a time, making it hard to find future & past events.
    - Month themes had harsh, inaccessible colors, making text hard to read. 
  </Fragment>
